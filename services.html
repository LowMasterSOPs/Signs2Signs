Our Services

What we make (and make well)

Built-up Acrylic Letters

Hand-crafted returns with CNC-cut acrylic faces — bonded, polished and finished for lightweight, premium 3D lettering. A sharp alternative to metal built-ups.

  • Hand-fabricated returns + CNC faces
  • Gloss, matt or special finishes
  • Depths from 30 mm upwards
  • Templates and fixings supplied
  • Weather-resistant and durable

ACM & Aluminium Sign Trays

ACM composite or aluminium, finished your way — powder-coated, vinyl-wrapped, or both. Add fret-cut lettering and LEDs for a tray that looks sharp day and night.

  • ACM composite or aluminium construction
  • Powder-coated or vinyl-wrapped finishes
  • Fret-cut & backed-off lettering options
  • LED illumination available
  • Supplied fully assembled, trade-ready

Illuminated & LED Signs

Face-lit, halo-lit, and built-up letters with LED panels, neon flex, or foam-embedded LEDs. Efficient, bright, and supplied with wiring guides for straightforward installs.

  • Face-lit, halo-lit & built-up letter options
  • Neon-style flex & foam-embedded LEDs
  • High-efficiency LED modules (5-year warranty)
  • IP65-rated weatherproofing
  • LED kits & wiring guides included

CNC Cut Letters & Logos

Sharp edges and clean finishes — precision cut in acrylic, aluminium, foamex and more, ready to fit with the right fixings.

  • Acrylic, aluminium, steel, brass
  • Thickness from 3 mm to 25 mm
  • Polished or brushed finishes
  • Mounting systems supplied
  • CAD file prep available

Vinyl & Large Format

High-res digital printing on premium vinyls for vehicles, walls, windows and wraps. Clean installs, strong colour, built to last.

  • UV-resistant inks & vinyls
  • Up to 1600 mm wide printing
  • Cut vinyl lettering & graphics
  • Application tape included
  • Lamination for extra durability
